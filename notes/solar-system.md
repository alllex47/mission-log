## Random facts

- Light from the Sun takes ~8 min 20 s to reach Earth, ~43 min to Jupiter, ~4.2 h to Neptune.
- More than 1,300 Earths would fit inside Jupiter.
- Pluto was reclassified as a dwarf planet in 2006. (i liked Pluto)
- The Sun holds ~99.8% of the solar system's mass.
